About Pep Band

The Philomath High School Pep Band brings energy and school spirit to our home athletic events. Made up of students from Jazz Band and Wind Ensemble, Pep Band performs at home football games during fall and basketball games during winter.

In addition to athletic events, Pep Band marches in the annual Homecoming Parade, showcasing Warrior pride throughout the community.

Pep Band provides a fun, informal performance opportunity where students can show their Warrior pride while entertaining crowds and supporting our athletes. From fight songs to popular tunes, Pep Band keeps the energy high at every game!
