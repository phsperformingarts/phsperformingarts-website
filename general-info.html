General Information

About Our Program

Welcome to the Philomath High School Performing Arts program! We are dedicated to providing exceptional music education and performance opportunities for our students.

Our program includes three main ensembles: Jazz Band, Choir, and Wind Ensemble. Students of all skill levels are encouraged to participate and grow as musicians.
