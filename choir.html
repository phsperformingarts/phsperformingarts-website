About Concert Choir

The Philomath High School Concert Choir provides students the opportunity to develop vocal technique, musicianship, and performance skills in a supportive ensemble environment.

Throughout the year, choir students participate in concerts and district competitions. District competitions are held in the spring, providing students the opportunity to perform for adjudicators, receive valuable feedback, and showcase their vocal abilities alongside other schools in the region.

Rehearsal Information

Concert Choir rehearses during the school day as part of the regular class schedule.

For more information, contact Ms. Erica Hall at erica.hall@philomath.k12.or.us
